# ACME Corp — Remote Work Policy (2026)

**Effective date:** January 1, 2026
**Policy owner:** Priya Nair, VP of People Operations
**Review cycle:** Annual

## 1. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day onboarding period are
eligible to work remotely. Contractors and interns require manager approval.

## 2. Remote work allowance
Eligible employees may work remotely up to **three (3) days per week**. Mondays and
Thursdays are designated company-wide in-office "collaboration days," so remote days
must be chosen from the remaining weekdays.

## 3. Equipment stipend
Each remote employee receives a one-time home-office stipend of **$750**, plus a
recurring **$40 per month** internet reimbursement. Receipts must be submitted through
the Expensify portal within 30 days of purchase.

## 4. Core hours
Regardless of location, employees must be available during core hours of
**10:00 AM to 4:00 PM in their local time zone**. Meetings should be scheduled within
these hours whenever possible.

## 5. Security requirements
Remote employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems, enable
full-disk encryption, and store no customer data on personal devices. Lost or stolen
hardware must be reported to security@acme.example within 24 hours.

## 6. Exceptions
Exceptions to the three-day limit (for example, fully remote roles) require written
approval from both the employee's manager and the VP of People Operations.
